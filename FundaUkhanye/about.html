Our Story

Funda Ukhanye began with a simple yet profound realization: many children in underserved communities like Ga-Rankuwa face significant hurdles in their educational journey, particularly concerning reading and academic challenges. These early struggles often lead to poor school performance and, in some cases, early school leaving.

Driven by a passion to make a tangible difference, our founders established Funda Ukhanye as a non-profit organization dedicated to breaking this cycle. We started with a small group of volunteers, providing after-school reading lessons and one-on-one tutoring. Over time, our programs expanded to include parent workshops, resource distribution, and now, even career guidance for older children. Our journey is one of continuous growth, fueled by the belief that every child, regardless of their background, deserves the opportunity to unlock their full potential and shine brightly.

Our Mission

Mission

To empower children through literacy and academic support by:
• Providing engaging reading programs that spark a love for books.
• Offering personalized tutoring to strengthen academic foundations.
• Mentoring children to build confidence and resilience in their learning journey.
• Partnering with schools, parents, and communities to create supportive environments for education.

Our Vision

Vision

To see every child in our community become a confident reader, lifelong learner, and shining example of what education can achieve.

Objectives & Outcomes

Core Objectives

  • Improve literacy skills among children aged 6 to 12
  • Provide consistent, accessible tutoring and mentorship
  • Foster positive attitudes towards learning and school attendance
  • Support academic progress to reduce dropout rates
  • Career guidance and varsity application assistance for ages 13-18

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved literacy and academic skills
  • Enhanced reading and comprehension abilities
  • Improved school attendance and performance
  • Increased self-confidence and motivation
  • Strengthened community involvement in education
