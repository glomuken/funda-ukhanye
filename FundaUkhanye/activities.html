Our Empowering Activities

Discover how we light young minds through diverse programs.

Our Key Programs

Weekly Reading Lessons

Focused on phonics, comprehension, and vocabulary, our weekly sessions build strong literacy foundations for children aged 6 to 12.

Personalized Tutoring Sessions

Tailored one-on-one and small group tutoring addresses individual academic needs across various subjects.

Parent & Caregiver Workshops

Workshops empower parents and caregivers with strategies and resources to support learning and reading at home.

Reading Materials Distribution

We provide age-appropriate books and educational resources to children and families to foster a love for reading.

Career Days

Inspiring career days expose older children (13-18) to various professions and future possibilities.

Varsity Application Assistance

Guidance and practical assistance for children aged 13-18 with tertiary institution applications and follow-ups.
