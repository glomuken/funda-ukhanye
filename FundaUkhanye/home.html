Our Work

Many children in underserved communities struggle with reading and academic challenges. Funda Ukhanye provides targeted reading instruction, tutoring, and mentorship to help children build strong foundations for lifelong learning.
• Weekly reading lessons (phonics, comprehension, vocabulary)
• One-on-one and group tutoring
• Mentorship and career guidance
• Workshops for parents & caregivers
• Distribution of books and resources

Our Solution

We offer engaging reading programs, personalized tutoring, and mentorship. By partnering with schools, parents, and communities, we create supportive environments for education and help children unlock their full potential.

Reading Lessons

Weekly sessions for ages 6-12 focusing on phonics, comprehension, and vocabulary.

Tutoring & Mentorship

One-on-one and group support, plus mentorship for academic and personal growth.

Career Guidance

Career days, varsity application help, and ongoing follow-up for teens (13-18).
